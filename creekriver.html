The Glorious Waterway - Creek River

Along the northeastern border of Eridanus lies the great Creek River: a periodically-filled waterway of average depth <1m. Although originally designated a creek when it was fully owned by the Commonwealth of Australia, the Republic officially designates it (much more correctly) as a river. By decision of the Glorious President Lieutenant General Vice Admiral Air Marshal on October 23rd 2024, and officially ratified in the Constitution of Eridanus, the entire river within the northwest and southeast border lines is owned by the Republic, and Australian territory begins on the north bank.

The river holds an important place in the hearts of the Eridanese people. A small, weak waterway, looked down upon by the tyrannical Australian government and people, designated as nothing more than a creek. It acts as a poetic metaphor for the situation of our burgeoning Republic, however, the river flourishes with just a bit of rain, and likewise the Republic will flourish thanks to the spirit of its people.

Tactically, the river denies access from the north-east except along the bridges, which are overlooked by Fort Cursa. Thus, the river acts as a very useful defensive landform.
