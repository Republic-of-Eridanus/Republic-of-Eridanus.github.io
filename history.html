Founding Day - October 23, 2024

On the 23rd of October 2024, at about 5pm, the current Glorious President Lieutenant General Vice Admiral Air Marshal planted the green and gold flag and officially declared the Republic of Eridanus independent from the tyranny of Australia.

Despite protests from authorities like the local council, and the difficulty of the terrain, the Glorious President Lieutenant General Vice Admiral Air Marshal pushed through, cutting his way past vines and ferns with his trusty knife, carrying the majestic green and gold flag toward victory.

After a long trek, the Glorious President Lieutenant General Vice Admiral Air Marshal arrived at the site of modern day Angetenar, planting the flag. Following this, he established the borders of the young Republic, surveyed the area, and planned key defensive points in case of a retaliation by the local council.
