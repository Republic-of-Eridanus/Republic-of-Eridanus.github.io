Forts and Defences of Eridanus

Following the proclamation of the republic on October 23rd, the Glorious President Lieutenant General Vice Admiral Air Marshal immediately got to work reinforcing the borders in case of attack, aware of the belligerent stature of local Australian authorities.

To protect the nation, there is one fort, 2 defensive lines and two military staging grounds in Archernar province alone. These locations are mostly natural, yet work hard to secure the nation's borders.

The east direction has two defensive lines, mainly due to the only road access being from that direction (and therefore, most likely military incursion). For this reason, there are two defensive lines that the army can use in the event of an invasion.
