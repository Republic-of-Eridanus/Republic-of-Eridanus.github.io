Landmarks of Eridanus

Despite being small, Eridanus still holds natural jewels within its borders.

One such natural wonder is Twin Rocks, a large, imposing landform located several metres from Angetenar. The sedimentary duo hold great importance to Eridanese in lands everywhere, acting as a great shield for the glorious capital and protecting it from all manners of evil and wrongdoing. The landform is revered, respected, even worshipped as one of natures greatest monuments, a testament to the power of the Earth, but also a sign that she cares deeply about her children. Additionally, Twin Rocks is in a convenient location to act as a defensive emplacement for Angetenar.

Along the northeastern border, the powerful force of Creek River creates an almost impassable obstacle, except for the conveniently placed Rana Bridge. The bridge is one of two crossings over the Creek River, and is overlooked by Fort Cursa (allowing the Army to halt any invaders who dare cross Rana into the Republic).
