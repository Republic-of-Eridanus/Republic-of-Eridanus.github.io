The Capital Of Eridanus - Angetenar

In the west of Archenar province lies the jewel of the Republic, the glorious capital, Angetenar.

Named after one of the stars in the celestial river Eridanus, Angetenar is a thriving city that acts as the beating heart of the Republic.

Eridanus is governed from Angetenar, and also contains the National Treasury where all assets of the Republic are kept.
