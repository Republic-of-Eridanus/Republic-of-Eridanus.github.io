Fun Facts about The Republic!

Did you know: The river which we call Creek River is, in Australia, considered a creek!

Did you know: The first flag of Eridanus was made with paint borrowed from the Glorious President Prime Minister Lieutenant General Vice Admiral Air Marshal's friend!
